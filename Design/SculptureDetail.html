The Narrative

This relief depicts Gajalakshmi, the goddess of prosperity, being bathed by divine elephants. Carved from a single block of premium red sandstone sourced from the Vindhya ranges, every detail—from the intricate folds of the goddess's drape to the majestic posture of the elephants—follows the strict iconographic canons of 12th-century Hoysala temple art.

The artisan has utilized a deep-relief technique, creating shadows that move across the stone as the ambient lighting changes throughout the day, bringing the ancient myth to life in a modern interior setting...
