CHRONICLES OF CREATION

From Rock to Masterpiece

Witness the metamorphosis of ancient stone into divine form. Each stroke of the hammer is a conversation between the artisan and the eternal soul of the earth.

1

Selection

Completed

The journey begins by identifying a stone with the right grain, density, and spiritual resonance for the intended deity or pattern.

2

Rough Shaping

Completed

Large chunks are removed to reveal the basic silhouette. This stage requires immense physical strength and a clear vision of the final proportions.

3

Detailed Carving

In Progress

The soul of the piece emerges. Artisans use smaller chisels to define eyes, jewelry, and intricate textures with mathematical precision.

4

Polishing

Upcoming

Using natural abrasives and water, the rough surface is smoothed to a satin finish, highlighting the natural beauty of the stone.

5

Blessing

Upcoming

The final step is the ritualistic awakening of the sculpture, where it is consecrated with offerings and prayers before its final journey.
